Recently, you may have noticed that there's a new 'Coming soon' category in the 'Life in the Plushie Kingdom' section. This is where the Government's next major service will be, and I'll provide some early details about it today.

## The problem a growing Kingdom faces

---

As the Plushie Kingdom expands, plushies who live on the outskirts of the Kingdom tend to get neglected compared to those living in more central areas. Difficulties with managing the whereabouts and details of plushies have also increased. This makes it harder for us to deliver services to plushies who need them, and makes personalising services to fit each plushie's needs impossible. To truly deliver real, meaningful change and enable all plushies to live life to the fullest, we need a way to keep track of all plushies in the Kingdom, storing their basic information in a comprehensive database.

## What this new service will be

---

After discussions with the other Government ministers, work has started on creating a live database that will store basic information about every single plushie living in the Kingdom. This service will be consistently updated as new plushies become Plushie Kingdom citizens to ensure all plushies are properly accounted for. The collection and storage of this data will also allow us to generate detailed statistics and infographics that deliver valuable insights on Plushie Kingdom citizens, enabling us to launch more useful, targeted information and services in the future.

## What building this service will involve

---

As you can see, building a live database for an entire Kingdom's citizens will be no easy feat. To make this service a reality, the Government will be tackling four major challenges:

### Code complexity

The Ministry of Technology will strive to ensure that this service is easy to maintain, and adheres to pk-gov's goal of being intuitive, informative and effective at giving plushies what they need from the Government. As this service is a large-scale project, we will only reveal more details about it in the coming months, so stay tuned to our <a class="link" href="/news/">Government news</a> page and <a class="link" href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCL4Px1LLyD6WxcaDTm71ViQ" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">YouTube channel</a> for more updates.

### Development and operation costs

As this service involves integrating several additional technologies into the pk-gov website, we want to ensure that this service is as cost-effective as possible, because Plushie Kingdom citizens shouldn't have to be economically burdened by the Government. Fortunately, careful planning and decision-making by the Ministry of Technology and the Ministry of Finance will allow us to launch this service without needing to pay a single cent!

### Privacy concerns

We understand that collecting every citizen's data may be concerning to some plushies. In building this service, we will ensure that only the most basic, necessary data is collected, such as each plushie's name, type and the date they joined the Kingdom. To ensure the safety of all plushies in the Kingdom, each plushie's place of residence will also be stored, to prevent plushies from being displaced or sent to the wrong location.

### Data accuracy

We want to ensure that data in this live database remains up-to-date at all times. In consideration of this, as well as our support for open access to information, all data in this live database will be publicly available to view at all times. This allows for greater transparency and for the Government to be notified of any inaccuracies. To protect the security of this data, only Government staff will be allowed to add new data or edit existing data in the database.

## What the future holds

---

This live database will become the benchmark for future Government efforts to improve life in the Kingdom. If this service is successfully implemented, it will demonstrate the Government's ability to complete large-scale projects and provide crucial insights needed to deliver more real, meaningful change to the Plushie Kingdom. We hope you're as excited for this as we are, and we can't wait to tell you more about this new service when it's closer to completion in the coming months!
