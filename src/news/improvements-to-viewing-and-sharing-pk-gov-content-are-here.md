To keep up with increasing demand and use of the pk-gov website, we're introducing more features designed to make the viewing and sharing experience of pk-gov even better.

## Table of Contents

---

![Picture showing table of contents for a page, in both desktop and mobile screen sizes](/assets/news-media/improvements-to-viewing-and-sharing-pk-gov-content-are-here/table-of-contents.webp)

Some pages on pk-gov can be tedious to navigate, containing various sections and long paragraphs. The new table of contents allows users to quickly jump to the section they're looking for, enabling faster and easier access to Government information. This feature will be available for all pages in the 'Life in the Plushie Kingdom' section, all Government news articles (like this one!) and the pages in the 'About us' section in the footer.

## Share pk-gov content with others

---

![Picture showing interface for sharing a page, in both desktop and mobile screen sizes](/assets/news-media/improvements-to-viewing-and-sharing-pk-gov-content-are-here/share-pages.webp)

As pk-gov gets more content and attention, it can be helpful to be able to share relevant pages with others to help them get the information they need. So, we've added a new share button that utilises your device's native share menu (or copies text to the clipboard if unsupported), allowing you to quickly share content with others. This feature will be available for all pages in the 'Life in the Plushie Kingdom' section and all Government news articles (like this one!).

## Government news page improvements

---

![Picture showing the difference in appearance between read and unread news articles](/assets/news-media/improvements-to-viewing-and-sharing-pk-gov-content-are-here/read-and-unread.webp)

As part of our efforts to improve the usability of pk-gov, news articles that have already been read will now be coloured light grey instead of the usual black, to help you tell which articles have been read and which ones haven't at a glance.

## Wrapping up

---

We hope these new features will enhance your everyday experiences on pk-gov. We'll continue to improve pk-gov with new features to make it the most intuitive, informative and effective Government resource it can be!
