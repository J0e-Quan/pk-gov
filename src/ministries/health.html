What we do

Provide quality healthcare

We treat plushies of any condition, giving them all the aid they need to return to a healthy life.

Support your mental health

With qualified experience in psychological assistance, you don't have to go through it alone. We're here to help you recover from deep cuts, both the physical and the mental kind.

Supply important medication

Stay healthy with regular medicine deliveries and vaccine injections. Contact your nearest Health Officer or Health Minister to learn more.
