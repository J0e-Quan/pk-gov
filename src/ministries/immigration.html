What we do

Welcome new plushies

We are responsible for handling the arrival of new plushies, including introducing them to the Kingdom and helping them settle in.

Secure our borders and biosecurity

We keep the Kingdom safe from contamination with strict biosecurity measures, ensuring all plushies enter the Kingdom with peace of mind.

Ensure no plushie is left out

We make sure all plushies are involved with life in the Kingdom, from community events to Government service access.
