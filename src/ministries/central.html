What we do

Oversee Government

We work with all other Government ministries to plan, coordinate and execute new policies. As the leading authority of the Government, we take great care in ensuring the Government works efficiently and effectively to improve plushies' lives.

Inform citizens

We are responsible for delivering important announcements on major developments in the Government and the Kingdom. These announcements are available here in the Government news page, as well as on our YouTube channel.
