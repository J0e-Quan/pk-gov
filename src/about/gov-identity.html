Government identity guidelines

Here's where to look for guidance on using the bold and modern, yet warm and welcoming identity of the Plushie Kingdom Government.

Colours

Background colour

For backgrounds of pages like this one, use pure white (#FFFFFF)

Text colours

Use either pure white (#FFFFFF) or pure black (#000000) depending on the background colour. Text coloured with the accent colours shown below isn't frequently used but isn't prohibited either.

Accent colours

We use a range of vibrant accent colours, each representing one of our ministries. Central Ministry blue represents both the ministry itself and the Government as a whole, symbolising its role as the leading authority of the Government. If text is shown on these colours, adjust the text's colour for better contrast and readability as shown below.

Central Ministry blue (#68A0FF)
Ministry of Foreign Affairs blue (#3653FF)
Ministry of Finance green (#00B000)
Ministry of Technology cyan (#4AFFD1)
Ministry of Food yellow (#FFDC00)
Ministry of Health red (#FF0000)
Ministry of Immigration brown (#C38C00)
Ministry of Defence grey (#A5A5A5)

Typography

Headers, Titles and Main Text

For major headlines, titles and other significant text, use IBM Plex Sans. For weights, we usually use the font in bold (700), but regular (400) can also be used for "lighter" titles and subheadings.

Body text

For body text, content, buttons and any other uses not mentioned for IBM Plex Sans, use Mona Sans. We use the font in its regular weight (400).

In writing

Key terms

Always write "Plushie Kingdom", "Kingdom", "Government", and ministry names in Title Case (first letter capitalised) when referring to these entities in writing.

Government members

When mentioning plushies who work in the Government, write their job title first, then their full name, all in Title Case, for example: Prime Minister Pink Bear Bear, Defence Minister Stitch

"Defence" or "Defense"?

Defence (unless you don't know what a kilometer is). Don't feel bad for not knowing, we only realised this after making all the ministry assets and had to redo some of them. (we even almost typed 'defense' in the example above :/)

"Plushie Kingdom Government", "Government" or "pk-gov"?

Only use the full term "Plushie Kingdom Government" in formal contexts, or mentioning it where other governments may be present. "Government" refers to the same entity anyways, and is shorter to write. "pk-gov" is NOT the same as "Plushie Kingdom Government" or "Government", as it is the platform to access Government information and services (the website and YouTube channel)
