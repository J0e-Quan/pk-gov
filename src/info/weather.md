## What is this service?

---

The Plushie Kingdom weather service provides up-to-date information on the latest weather conditions in the greater Plushie Kingdom region and the surrounding George Town area. Severe weather alerts are NOT delivered here, those will be posted in the <a class="link" href="/news/">Government news</a> page.

## Alternative sources of weather information

---

Although we strive to ensure that weather information provided by this service is as accurate as possible, it always helps to get a second (or third) source of information. These are some other resources that provide local weather information:
