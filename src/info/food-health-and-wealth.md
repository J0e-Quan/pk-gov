## Air as food?

---

It may be tasteless, colourless and not smell nice, but it's available anywhere in the greater Plushie Kingdom region! Air has been a staple food in the Plushie Kingdom since the beginning, and was the only food option until recently, when the Ministry of Food brought in several new types of food for all plushies to enjoy!

## Food in the Plushie Kingdom

---

The Plushie Kingdom has a large variety of dining options available, most of which are located in the Big Tent.

![Picture of food options available in the Plushie Kingdom](/assets/info-media/food-health-and-wealth/food-options.webp)
Meals are available from vendors such as Rooty and Bearista, who provide an all-in-one stop for both food and drinks. You can also pick from several individual food items like Sushi and Egg to create your own perfect breakfast, lunch, dinner, or anything else in between!

## Healthcare services

---

The Plushie Kingdom offers quality healthcare from sickness to recovery and beyond in the entire greater Plushie Kingdom region at no cost, so that no sickness or health condition can stop Plushie Kingdom citizens from living life to the fullest! For general treatments, medical checkups and advice, there are Health Officers available in the Big Tent and the Studio. Plushies with more serious conditions are in need of more specialised treatment may contact the Health Minister or Deputy Health Minister for further assistance. All Ministry of Health staff members are also capable of providing support and counselling for mental health problems to make sure that all plushies remain healthy both physically and mentally!

## Financial services

---

The Plushie Kingdom is home to world-class banking expertise, ensuring the financial health of all plushies remains secure. All financial activity, including investments, loans and bank accounts are handled and regulated by the Ministry of Finance. To access or learn more about these services, contact your nearest Ministry of Finance staff member.
